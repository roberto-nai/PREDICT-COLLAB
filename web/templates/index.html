Welcome to Predict-Collab!

Logs:

In this section, you can create a new process, associate it with a log in CSV or XES format, and add a custom description. Additionally, it offers the ability to manage uploaded logs, allowing you to delete, download, or select them to proceed with creating and training a model.

Models:

In this section you can create and train a new model by selecting a log, choose what type of prediction you want to make and select which columns will be taken for the prediction. It offers the possibility to manage the models created, allowing you to delete them, view their evaluation metrics or select them to go to make a prediction.

Predictions:

In this section you can perform the prediction of your traces. By selecting a process and its associated log, all available prediction types will be listed, followed by the models associated with those types. Then, simply load the trace for which you wish to perform the prediction.
